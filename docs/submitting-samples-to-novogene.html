Chapter 11 Submitting samples to Novogene

  1. Log in to https://cssamerica.novogene.com/
  • Username: jtabima@clarku.edu
  • Password: Basidiobolus666

  1. Click on Send Samples

  2. Select the project and click on add a new batch

  3. Fill basic info with Javier’s info

  4. In sample summary, make sure that all your tubes are 1.5mL or we will have to pay more $

  5. Download the template, fill it up and then upload it

  6. In transportation Condition:

  • If youre sending HMW DNA or RNA: Dry Ice
  • Anything else: Ice Packs

  1. Courier provider: FedEx (No tracking number), check everything else and submit the SIF

  2. Print the SIF and include it in the package
